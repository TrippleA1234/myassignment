1 var has the function or global scope. let's have the block scope. const variable has the block scope.
2 It gets hoisted to the top of its scope and initialized undefined. It also got hoisted to the top of its scope but didn't initialize. It also got hoisted to the top of its scope but didn't initialize.
3 It can be updated or re-declared. It can only be updated and can't be re-declared. It can't be updated or re-declared.
